Buy and Sell Real Estate Properties

The real estate market is picking up in Lucknow with heightened business and economic growth and prosperity of the city. Demand is high in areas like Hazratganj, Amar Shaheed Path, Gomti Nagar, Faizabad Road, Raebareli Road, Gomti Nagar Extension, Sultanpur Road and the Sitapur National Highway. Property rates have already gone up in these destinations and they offer good returns to investors and property buyers. Daliganj, Aliganj, Niralanagar and Mahanagar also offer good returns with demand touching unbelievable levels in these areas. Jankipuram and Jankipuram Extension are other areas which are witnessing a huge rise in demand. Sushant Golf City, located at the Lucknow-Sultanpur Road, is a hi-tech city township which is being built over an area of 3,530 acres. Omaxe City on Amar Shaheed Path spread over an area of 14-acre, Eldeco City, DLF Garden City, One Place City and Sahara City Homes are some of the integrated townships which are soon to be a part of the city. The Registrars Office does property registration. Property registration charges were raised in 2009 and have to be paid depending on the stamp duty. The Lucknow Nagar Nigam (LNN) is responsible for the collection of property taxes in the city.

Steps to buying a property in Lucknow
Here are a few things you must consider before buying a property in Lucknow.
1. Your budget Fixing a budget should be your primary step when buying a property in Lucknow. This will help you determine a suitable property within your purchasing power. It will also help you decide whether you require a home loan or can finance the purchase yourself. Also, remember to keep track of the additional charges such as brokerage fees, property tax, legal fees, stamp duty, and more as they can add up to a considerable sum.
2. Type of property You must be clear about the type of property you wish to buy and the purpose of purchase. Have a clear idea of whether you need to move into a big place with your family or buy a house just for yourself. This will help you narrow down the choices and find a place that is worth investing in.
3. Credibility of builder Another thing to consider when buying a property is the builder’s credibility. You must run background checks and find out as much as you can about the builder and their previous work before investing in a property. After all, the builder’s reputation, along with past successes and failures, could affect your current purchase. Moreover, it could boost your home loan eligibility when applying for housing finance.
4. Location When buying property, it is significant to choose a location that checks the right boxes and fulfils all your needs. If you wish to live away from the city’s hustle and bustle, a quiet suburban locality will work well. Whereas, if you need a property closer to your workplace, then a flat in the heart of the city might be the better choice. Some other factors to consider are safety, hygiene, connectivity, and proximity to important places like schools, hospitals, etc.
5. Housing finance If you are struggling to finance your property purchase in a city like Lucknow, don’t be disheartened. Availing of a home loan in Lucknow can help you buy the house of your choice without exhausting your savings. In fact, many lenders now provide housing finance solutions at affordable home loan interest rates, ensuring your buying journey remains hassle-free!
